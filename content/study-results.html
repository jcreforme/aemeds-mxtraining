Results of a 2-year clinical study in patients with chronic migraine

Study goal

Study the safety of VYEPTI 300 mg and patient-reported results over 2 years.

People in the study

128 people with chronic migraine.

Dosing

Everyone in the study knew they were receiving VYEPTI 300 mg, which may have enhanced the results.

Safety results were similar to the original clinical trials

More than 70% reported they felt an overall improvement in their migraine over 21 months

Based on a question asking people how they feel about change in their overall migraine status, ranging from very much worse/much worse to much improved/very much improved. Improvement includes both much improved and very much improved responses.

Because of the way these data were collected, we cannot make any conclusions about how well VYEPTI works based on these results.

Real VYEPTI patient pictured was compensated for her time. She did not participate in the study.
